Our Story

BORN FROM FIRE

Mova started in Tbilisi in 2018 with one simple obsession: the perfect burger. What began as a weekend pop-up grew into a full delivery empire built on the same values — quality, speed, and flavor that hits different.

We source everything locally, grind our own beef fresh daily, and bake our buns in-house. Every shawarma is carved from halal meat marinated overnight. Every salad is chopped to order.

✓ Locally sourced ingredients
✓ Freshly ground beef daily
✓ No frozen products ever
